SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Photo Guidelines

File Type

You can upload any .jpeg, .jpg, .png, or other photo format. Please ensure your photo is not blurry and is well-lit. We will not accept a picture of a computer screen.

Quality

We can accept photos of any quality, but be aware that we cannot edit the photo to be more clear or sharp. The higher quality that you send to us, the better your light will look.

→

Logo Guidelines

File Type

We prefer the original vector file for your logo — most commonly .SVG, .AI, or .PDF. If you only have a .jpg, .jpeg, or other non-vector file, additional charges may apply depending on complexity. We will not accept a picture of a computer screen.

Quality

We can accept logos of any quality, but be aware that we cannot edit the logo to be more clear or sharp. The higher quality you send us, the better your light will look.

→

Design Guidelines

File Type

We prefer the original vector file — most commonly .SVG, .AI, or .PDF. If you only have a non-vector file, additional charges may apply. We will not accept a picture of a computer screen. You can also send us an idea for a design you'd like us to create — additional charges may apply.

Quality

We can accept designs of any quality, but we cannot edit them to increase clarity. The higher quality you send us, the better your light will look.
