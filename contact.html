Frequently Asked Questions

Custom Photo LED Lights: You can upload any .jpeg, .jpg, .png, or other photo format. Please ensure your photo is not blurry and is well-lit.

Custom Logo LED Lights: We prefer to have the original .svg, .ai, or other vector files. If you only have a .jpg, .jpeg, or other non-vector file, additional charges may apply. We will not accept a picture of a computer screen.

Custom Design LED Lights: We prefer the original .svg, .ai, or other vector files. If you only have a non-vector file, additional charges may apply. You can also send us an idea for a design you'd like us to create.

We can use photos of any quality to create your light, however, be aware that the higher quality the photo is, the better your light will look. We cannot edit any photos to increase the sharpness or clarity. If the original photo is blurry, the light will also look blurry in the same places.

If you have questions about whether a specific design will work, please email us at led62.info@gmail.com. We're happy to take a look before you commit to an order.
