About Us

LED62 started with a simple idea — what if you could turn your favorite photo, your team's logo, or a design that means something to you into a glowing piece of art? Based out of Plant City, Florida, we're a small business that does exactly that. Every single light is made by us, in-house, with a laser engraver and a whole lot of care.

We're not a big box store. We don't mass produce. When you order from LED62, a real person is engraving your design, checking the quality, and packing it up for you. That's something we're genuinely proud of.

Made In-House

Every light is laser-engraved right here in Plant City, FL. No outsourcing, no middlemen — just us making sure every order is something you'll love.

Unique & Personal

Your light is made for you and nobody else. Whether it's a family photo, a sports logo, or a character you love — we bring it to life in glowing acrylic.

Customer First

We're a small business and our reputation means everything to us. If something isn't right, we make it right. Simple as that.
