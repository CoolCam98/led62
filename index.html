LIGHT IT UP

Easy to Use Base

Plug it in, or use three AA batteries

Every Lumi LED light comes with a color-changing LED base, micro-USB cable, and remote control. Setup takes seconds — just place your acrylic design in the base and power it on.

WIRED OR WIRELESS

Power via the included micro-USB cable, or use three AA batteries (not included) for a completely cord-free setup.

SIMPLE SETUP

Peel off any protective paper from the design, place the acrylic piece into the base, power it on, and you're done. It really is that simple.

EASY CONTROL

Use the included remote to choose from 16 different colors and patterns at the touch of a button. Or use the button directly on the base.
